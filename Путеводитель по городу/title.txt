It is not without reason that Kyrgyzstan is called the “Asian Shchitsevariya” - a third of the country's territory is occupied by the Tien Shan dawns with snow caps and green meadows - pastures.You need to travel around Kyrgyzstan without stopping for a long time in cities to see mirror lakes, giant valleys, transparent streams and stormy waterfalls, mountain gorges, nomad yurts and bright flowers on the slopes.
