There are quite a lot of petroglyphs of various eras and cultures in Kyrgyzstan.  A complete list of “painted” places can be found here.  - rock paintings Near the main resort of Issyk-Kul, the town of Cholpon-Ata - there is a very impressive place - a tract of thousands of stones on 42 hectares of land.  All stones are covered with drawings from the period of the beginning of 2 thousand BC.  - 7th century A.D.  They depict animal scenes of hunting, festivals and wars in different ways.  To this day, it is not clear exactly why so many stones covered with drawings were collected in one place.
