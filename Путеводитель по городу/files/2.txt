The giant market stretches for a kilometer along the western bank of the Ak-Bura River.  Kyrgyz, Uzbek, Chinese and Tajik merchants flock here.  They sell real oriental food, spices, household items, clothes.  At the same time, the ranks of artisans are especially curious, as well as food stalls bursting with delicacies and cheap seasonal fruits.  The market is open all week, most active on Sunday morning.
